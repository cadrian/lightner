About Lightner

A simple Leitner box, with an obvious pun.

The idea comes from this video (in French): Science Étonnante — MIEUX APPRENDRE & ÉTUDIER : les (vraies) techniques scientifiques.

Currently, Lightner supports the following cards types:

  • Texts
  • Web links (useful for quizzes)
  • Images
  • Audio (soon)
  • Video (soonish)
  • Mind maps (some day)
